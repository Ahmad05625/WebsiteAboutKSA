Discover Saudi Arabia

Learn more about the culture and history of Saudi Arabia on the official Saudi government website.

Saudi Arabia is a country located in the Middle East, known for its rich history, culture, and the birthplace of Islam. It is home to the two holiest cities in Islam, Makkah and Madinah.

The kingdom's economy is largely driven by oil production, but in recent years, Saudi Arabia has been diversifying its economy with initiatives like Vision 2030, which aims to reduce the country's dependency on oil and develop other sectors like tourism and entertainment. This vision also emphasizes technological advancements and environmental sustainability.

Saudi Arabia is also famous for its stunning desert landscapes, the Rub' al Khali (Empty Quarter), and modern cities like Riyadh and Jeddah. The country offers a mix of traditional culture and modern development, making it a fascinating destination for visitors. It is truly a place of contrasts.
